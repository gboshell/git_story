Chapter 1

He started to type. It was not even nine yet but it felt much later. Assignments were piling up and he
hadn’t had a day off since the beginning of the semester. I stopped, he had been sweating, he guessed
he smelled and decided to check; yes, it was bad. He wanted to take a shower but couldn’t find a
time slot to allot to a task that would take fifteen minutes but would distract him from his endless typing.

He typed some of it he understood, some he didn’t, some things worked for him, some not.
He promised himself that he would catch up later and make sure he understood it all but in the back of
his mind he knew he wouldn’t, who had the time for that, all he had time for was typing these days,
the learning he could do later.
