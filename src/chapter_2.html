Chapter 1

He was typing again, it had been a week since he had to type something like this. Of course, it came
back to him, lightning fast, he got back into the flow of things. Things had been going better, his
hygiene was much improved, his work- life balance, he thought was improving too. But, there was
always the typing. He believed that one day it would stop, but that day was far off, so far that it was
better not to think of it. He pushed it to the back of his mind.
