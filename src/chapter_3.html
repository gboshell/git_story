Chapter 2

It had been a long weekend, a bank holiday, but he had to work. He took two of the days off, he
deserved it, midterm was a long way off and he didn’t want to get burnt out. The last day he worked.
He worked from early the morning to late at night. He only took brakes t rest his eyes and eat his
dinner. He showered, he promised himself that he would try to stay clean, he had to re-join the
normal world eventually. He hoped it would be soon.

His PC died today. He tried to save it, but it was gone. Luckily, he had backed up his college work and
photos. Lost were some three hundred movies and some thirteen thousand songs, collected over
the years, but nothing that couldn’t be replaced. So now he just had to move some stuff around, his
laptop was brand new and would serve until he could get a replacement, He was surprisingly upbeat
about the whole thing.

He didn’t mind the typing now. He connected his laptop up to his PC screen, it made his work a little
easier, he wondered why he hadn’t done it before, the novelty brought brighter feeling to the day.
He thought , maybe things will get better, no, he knew they would.
